八、数据保留 / Data Retention

我们在实现服务目的所需期间保留信息。不同数据类型保留期限可能不同。

  • 备份数据：最多保留 10 天，且在保留窗口内不可恢复。

We retain data for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. Backup copies are retained for up to 10 days and are not restorable during the retention window.

You can request deletion in-app via “Settings > Delete Account”. We immediately delete production data directly associated with your account, including merchant profile data, inventory, images, try-on outputs, and related points records. This action is irreversible.

为履行法律义务、防欺诈和安全审计，我们可能在合法范围内保留最小必要记录；备份最多保留 10 天。

We may retain minimum records where required for legal compliance, anti-abuse, and security auditing, and maintain non-restorable backups for up to 10 days.

如您无法通过应用内完成删除请求，也可以通过联系邮箱提交请求。为保护账号安全，我们可能要求您提供 合理的身份验证信息。
